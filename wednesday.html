Jellystone

On Wednesday, families will arrive and can take part in the many fun activities at Jellystone. There is a jumping pillow, a 300 foot waterslide, mini golf, 2 huge pools, a splash pad, volleyball, horseshoes, basketball, fishing, outdoor movies every friday, hey hey hey rides, crafts, golf cart rentals, and playgrounds. Each week is themed something diffferent and the week we are going on is Happy Birthday USA! For more about the activities and events at Jellystone check out the Brochure and the Calendar. Directions

Latitude - N 37.8.043 Longitude - W 85.59.718 or Travel I-65 south from Louisville, KY or I-65 north from Nashville, TN to exit 53, Cave City, KY. Take Hwy 70 West toward Mammoth Cave National Park for one mile to the JELLYSTONE PARK™ entrance. Look for Yogi Bear™ waiting at the entrance to greet you!

Food

On Wednesday, no food will be provided until dinner, where we will be having a nacho bar including chips, taco meat, cheese sauce, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, jalapeno slices, olives, black beans, lettuce, onions, tomatos, and brownie bites for dessert. There will also be a fruit drink and water.
