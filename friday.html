Kentucky Action Park

Today we will be spending the day at Kentucky Action Park. Here are some of the attractons at Kentucky Action Park. We will be going there after breakfast and returning before dinner. For all of the attractions visit this webpage. You can do activities per ticket, or buy a wristband that gives you unlimited times on everything! (excluding the horseback riding, canoe riding, and ziplines, which cost extra) You can stay as long as you would like at Kentucky Action Park.

Alpine Slide/Chairlift

The Alpine slide at Kentucky Action Park is the only Alpine Slide in Kentucky! You must be at least 6 years old and you need to be able to control your speed racing down the twin tracks. The Alpine Slide is accessible through riding the chairlift. You can ride the chairlift up and back without doing the Alpine Slide if you desire.
