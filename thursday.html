Food

Breakfast

For breakfast we will be having bagels with toppings such as jam and cream cheese. There will also be milk and a fruit provided.

Lunch

For lunch we will be having peanut butter and jelly (or honey) sandwiches with chips, carrots, fruit, and cookies. Juice will also be provided. Lunch will be packed and eaten in between cave tours on the National Park grounds.

Dinner

For dinner we will be having pulled pork sandwiches, salad, watermelon, and dessert of ice cream sandwiches. There will also be a drink provided

Mammoth Caves

Most of the day Thursday will be spent in Mammoth Caves National Park. You are encouraged to do the Historic Tour at 10:00am and the Domes and Dripstones tour at 2:00pm. We will have lunch between tours and will have an oppertunity to look through the visitors center.
